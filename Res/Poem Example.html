THE JOURNEY

The road is long and the hound he is with me now He leads through the labyrinth of the spiraling quest My steps they are sure, confident I proceed In search of the centre, my staff in my hand On my back is my harp, the bridge between all the worlds The sign of my skillful ways in the realms of the soul The creatures of wood and dale, the doves of the upper air All come to my beck and call, assist me this life My past re-incarnations, hold no fear shame or glory For they are all lessons learned, assisting me now The few rocks in my path, are now easy to traverse For my love of The Goddess shines the way through the dark I vision the gate ahead, far greater than those behind And eagerly I stride on, strong and sure is my will For I know I will meet Her there. The One with the Starry Crown The Lady of Constant Bliss, Contentment and Joy

Donovan Leitch Written from Card Twenty Two Shapeshifter Tarot Llewellyn Publications Mallorca 15/7/2011
